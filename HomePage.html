WELCOME TO SAUDI ARABIA.

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is a Middle Eastern country known for its rich history, culture, and significant role in the global economy. It has diverse landscapes, including deserts and coastal areas. With a conservative interpretation of Islam, it is home to the holy cities of Mecca and Medina. Saudi Arabia heavily relies on oil exports but is working towards diversifying its economy. The country offers historical sites, stunning natural wonders, and is actively promoting tourism. Saudi Arabian society is rooted in conservative traditions, with ongoing changes in women's rights and roles.
